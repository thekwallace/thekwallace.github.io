Who’s Winning — Privacy Policy

Effective July 5, 2026

The short version

Your data stays on your device. Who’s Winning collects nothing, tracks nothing, and has no servers of its own.

What the app stores

Players, series, matches, and scores you enter are stored locally on your device. Player names and optional phone numbers exist only so the app can label scores and pre-address score texts; they are never transmitted to us.

What the app does not do

  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • No account creation and no data collection by the developer.
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties.

Texting scores

When you text scores or linking invitations, the message is composed and sent from your own Messages app, under your control. Nothing is sent on your behalf.

Game Center (optional)

You may optionally sign in to Apple’s Game Center to identify players. This is handled entirely by Apple under Apple’s privacy policy; the app stores the resulting player identifier and nickname on your device only.

Widgets & Live Activities

Lock-screen widgets and Live Activities read scores from shared storage on your device. They do not send data anywhere.

Changes & contact

If a future version adds optional sync or sharing features, this policy will be updated before those features ship. Questions: kwallace@ramblinoaks.com.
