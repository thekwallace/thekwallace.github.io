Questions, bug reports, or feature ideas: email support@kwallace.com. TestFlight users can also send feedback straight from TestFlight.

Common questions

How do I fix a score? Open the match, swipe left on the round to delete it, and re-enter it.

I started a match by mistake. Open the match, tap “…” → Discard Match.

Can I delete a finished match? Yes — open it, tap “…” → Delete Match. It asks twice on purpose: deleting rewrites the series lifetime totals.

Where is my data stored? On your device. See the privacy policy.

Is Game Center required? No. Signing in is optional and only used to identify players.
